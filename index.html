01 — About

Biography.

Shayan is a junior at Noor Ul Iman School in Princeton, NJ, enrolled in the highest-level courses offered — while concurrently earning college credit through dual enrollment at Middlesex College.

He is the founder and executive director of Syntra, a community health nonprofit delivering pop-up clinics and programs to underserved areas, and the founder of Verity, an AI-powered emergency companion application currently in alpha development.

His research spans neuroscience, genomics, and biotechnology. He has been recognized with the Gold Presidential Volunteer Service Award and multiple academic honors.
